    Invent the World

    In 2014, Binary Gambit was contracted to develop a series of Minecraft levels for Invent the World, which is an educational holiday program aimed at kids who love Minecraft.

    The levels are designed to encourage teamwork, critical thinking and to teach proper online behavior.

    You are welcome to download and try the levels via the Technic Modpack Launcher for free.

    YOUNG CRAFTERS - For kids or parents new to Minecraft

    ZEROSUM - Economy, Trading and Social Skills

    GOLDTRAIN - Goal-based Team Construction

    TO THE MOON - Complex recipes
