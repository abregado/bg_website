    Benifits of Rapid Prototyping?

    Having a game developed can cost 100’s of thousands of dollars, and there is no guarantee that a concept will actually take off. Professionals in the games industry use a technique known as Rapid Prototyping to mitigate these risks.

    Rapid Prototypes are not full saleable products. They are playable, but only enough to prove or disprove the worth of a design. These can take as little as 2 days to build, which means you save money while finding out if you want to invest in a full scale production.

    If you decide you want to fund your game in full, having a Prototype to show a production house will save you a lot of time when you try to explain your idea. It also makes it much faster to develop a full product, which means you save even more money.
