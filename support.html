    How can I support Binary Gambit?

    We don’t want anything for free! If you want to support us there are a number of things you can do:

    Buy one of our games

    This sounds obvious, but buying an extra copy of a game for a friend, or purchasing one of our games you are not super interested in helps us a lot. It boosts our stats and gives us more motivation to make great games!

    Donate to a prototype

    You can also “pay what you want” for your favorite free prototype on Itch.io. This helps us know which prototypes are the most popular so we can make a better choice on what to develop next.

    Subscribe for email updates, Twitter or Facebook

    Want to know when we release new prototypes or games? Looking for oportunities to be a last minute tester? Sign up, Subscribe or Follow us.
