    TravelBonsai

    A unique procedurally generated, pixel art Bonsai tree to call your own

    The latest Binary Gambit prototype in the works, TravelBonsai will be a relaxing mix of pet manager and horticulture simulation.

    As a new digital Bonsai Master, you will shape the growth of a unique tree, from seed to fruit. Play your way. Will you let nature run wild, or will you train your Bonsai like a living, pixel art sculpture?

    The TravelBonsai prototype will be released for free on itch.io and Google Play. If it generates enough interest then a full market version will be developed.

    Features

    Unique Seed
    Your bonsai will literally be one of a kind, from the moment it sprouts, to the moment you trade it away
    A very Zen Gift
    Once you have your Bonsai the way you want, share it over Social Media, or give it a new owner
    Realistic, but Managable Physics
    TravelBonsai has a simulation that mimics real life growth of trees, except where it would interfere with the gameplay. Your tree will never need watering, and never die
    Tools of the Trade
    Utilize a wide range of tools to shape your Bonsai. No unlocking means you can begin customsing your tree as soon as it is planted
    Beautiful Art
    Your Bonsai will grow into a beautiful work of art. Everything about it will be unique
    Multi-platform
    Purchase on itch.io and recieve a Bonsai on your Desktop PC as well
